ABOUT ONYX

Onyx is a litigation firm set in Delhi. Trained in various law firms and litigation chambers across the country, Onyx’s members offer a wide range of experience and expertise in the litigation space, as opposed to traditional law firms. Onyx is a recognition of client feedback on the need to provide comprehensive and professional services in the space of traditional litigation.

While Onyx operates as a law firm, its partners are closely involved through all the stages of the dispute till its resolution. The partners themselves file and argue matters and / or engage and brief senior advocates as needed. This allows clients to retain the services of Onyx singularly, without having to engage multiple professionals for the same matter.

FORMATION

Bhargavi, Priyanka and Siddhant met at their alma mater, NALSAR University of Law and later joined forces as colleagues in a Tier-1 law firm in Mumbai. They later ventured into independent litigation, having relocated to Delhi. Now, these seasoned lawyers reunite and merge practices to broaden their expertise, elevate client service and leverage their wealth of shared experience.

Onyx Chambers was born from this synergy of their journeys.

CLIENT PHILOSOPHY

Onyx’s professionals are trained across various institutions, platforms and offices across India. Its members recognize client requirements across the board, from individuals; founder and promoter-driven organizations; small and medium enterprises; to large multinationals and conglomerates.

The Chamber’s aim is to provide tailor-made solutions to its clients, by taking into account cost factors and suitability of legal service. Onyx strives to uphold the highest standards for each client, and endeavours to create long-lasting relationships. Its members aim to provide efficient, cost effective and long term solutions for their clients, while balancing their duties as officers of the court.
