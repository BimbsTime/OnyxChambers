Bhargavi Kannan

Partner

Bhargavi Kannan is a founding partner at Onyx Chambers. She specializes in civil litigation, arbitration, and corporate advisory. With over a decade of experience, Bhargavi has represented clients before the Supreme Court of India, various High Courts, and tribunals across the country.

Prior to founding Onyx Chambers, Bhargavi worked at a Tier-1 law firm in Mumbai and later established her independent practice in Delhi. She has extensive experience in handling complex commercial disputes, constitutional matters, and regulatory issues.

Bhargavi graduated from NALSAR University of Law and is enrolled with the Bar Council of Delhi. She is known for her strategic approach to litigation and her ability to provide practical solutions to complex legal problems.

Priyanka Vora

Partner

Priyanka Vora is an experienced litigation advocate with a strong background in commercial disputes, white collar crime disputes, constitutional law, and civil law. A graduate of NALSAR University of Law, she has 12 years of expertise in litigation, recognized for strategic counsel, high-stakes case drafting, and arguing or briefing senior advocates in the Supreme Court of India, High Courts, and company law tribunals.

She has been enrolled as an advocate since May 2013, and is qualified as an advocate-on-record at the Supreme Court of India since July 2022.

Priyanka has handled significant cases including validity of arbitration agreements that are not stamped (7-judge bench of the Supreme Court), constitutional validity of the Insolvency & Bankruptcy Code in respect of limitation, personal guarantors of corporate debtors and avoidance applications, as well as high-value corporate amalgamations. She has represented clients in various high-profile litigations in the Supreme Court, high courts and company law tribunals.

Siddhant Buxy

Partner

Siddhant Buxy is a dispute resolution lawyer based in New Delhi. His practice expanses across the Supreme Court of India, the Delhi High Court, the Principal Benches of NCLAT, NCLT and NGT, the Patiala House Court, and Arbitrations. His practice includes client advisory, litigation strategy, drafting and filing cases, and arguing or briefing senior advocates for hearings. He is also routinely engaged as a counsel by solicitor firms.

Siddhant's practice covers a diverse range of laws. He has appeared in various reported judgments on arbitration law, insolvency law, mining, taxation, securities and company law, environmental law, tenders / government contracts and constitutional law. His has represented a wide range of clients including a large corporate house, listed companies, a Non-Banking Financial Institution, Navratna PSUs, a major sports administration body, Indian subsidiary of a major Japanese company, MSMEs, a state government, private medical colleges, MPs and MLAs, high ranking civil servants, HNIs, commercial pilots, and indigent persons. He has also assisted in research and editing of authoritative treatises on Company law, Contract law and Arbitration law.

Siddhant graduated from the NALSAR University of Law, Hyderabad in 2013 with University Gold Medal in Trial and Appellate Advocacy and Professional Ethics. He was enrolled as an advocate in May 2013. He has been qualified to file and appear before the Supreme Court of India as an Advocate-on-Record since July 2022.
